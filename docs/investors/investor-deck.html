06

Competitive Landscape

The market has solutions — but none built voice-first for the people who need help most.

Pill Reminder Apps

Require smartphone literacy, manual data entry, and ongoing engagement. Low retention among seniors.

Smart Pill Dispensers

Hardware-only, no AI intelligence, limited to dispensing — can't capture health data or answer questions.

Care Navigation Firms

Human-intensive, expensive to scale. Can only handle so many patients per coordinator.

PBM Programs

Carrier-driven adherence programs are rigid, lack personalization, and serve the PBM's interests first.

AdherePod's edge: Voice-native AI that enhances existing programs — not a replacement, but a force multiplier for care teams, employers, and patients alike.
