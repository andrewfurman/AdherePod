Clean, professional look. Full custom branding (Pantone colors, laser-cut logos). Best for customer-facing deployments.

- [Armodilo Desktop Tablet Stands](https://armodilo.com/collections/desktop-tablet-stands)
- [Armodilo Sphere (desktop POS-style)](https://armodilo.com/products/sphere)
- [Custom Branding Options](https://armodilo.com/collections/customizations)
- [Custom Tablet Enclosures](https://armodilo.com/better-by-design/custom-tablet-enclosures/)
- Uses swappable "Fit Kits" for different tablet models
- In-house paint booth, CAD/CAM, 3D printing, laser cutting

### Mid-Range: Bouncepad (~$80-150)

Healthcare-focused, tamper-proof. Pre-designed for healthcare environments.

- [Bouncepad — Samsung Galaxy Tab](https://bouncepad.com/pages/samsung-galaxy-tab)

## Recommendation

**For prototyping now:** Grab a cheap Amazon stand (~$30) + a vinyl logo sticker ($1-2). Gets you a demo device fast.

**For pilot (5-10 units):** Bosstab or CTA Digital (~$50-80). Clean look, cable management, reasonable price.

**For scale (50+ units):** Armodilo or Bouncepad with custom branding. Negotiate bulk pricing.
