Company Overview & Product

Q: What does AdherePod do, and what unmet medical need does it address?

AdherePod is a voice native, AI powered medication adherence platform built for elderly and low literacy populations. The core product is a pre-configured, LTE connected tablet that sits in a patient's home. Patients simply speak to it: "AdherePod, what pills do I take this morning?" No apps to download, no screens to navigate, no digital literacy required.

The unmet need is massive: 50% of chronic disease patients don't take medications correctly. 1 in 4 ER visits is caused by medication errors. This costs the U.S. healthcare system $528 billion annually. Existing solutions (pill reminder apps, smart dispensers, care navigators) all fail to reach the most vulnerable patients because they require smartphone literacy, manual setup, or expensive human labor.

Q: What features does the product have today?
  • Voice AI agent: Real time conversational medication management powered by OpenAI (WebRTC, sub-second response)
  • Camera intake: Hold up a prescription bottle or pharmacy label and the device reads it automatically (Google Gemini vision)
  • Medication management: Add, edit, track all medications with dosing schedules, interaction checking, and reminders
  • Provider dashboard: Clinicians see real time adherence data, conversation transcripts, and can add clinical notes
  • Email reminders: Automated per-medication reminders and daily summaries
  • Multi-role system: Patient, provider, and admin roles with proper authorization

The product is live at adherepod.com with 30 automated end-to-end tests and continuous deployment. READY
